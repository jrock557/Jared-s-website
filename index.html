Share Your Stuff

Reduce waste and support families in need by donating your gently-used furniture.

Our Mission

We're tackling furniture waste by connecting people who have extra household items with families who need them. It's simple: donate, share, and make an impact.

How It Works

1. You list your available furniture.
2. We coordinate with partner organizations and churches.
3. The items get picked up and delivered to those who need them most.

Get Involved

Whether you're donating, volunteering, or partnering as a local organization, your involvement helps make our community stronger and more sustainable.
